How does it work?

  • Step One

    Upload

    To upload your dataset you have to be registered. Prepare your files in the supported CSV file format. Upload your own animal movement dataset.

  • Step Two

    Feature Extraction

    From your dataset various features are extracted and calculated. For instance speed of each animal, convex hull of the animal group or the triangulation of the swarm. This step takes some time.

  • Step Three

    Explore

    Visualize the movement dataset and the extracted features in multiple views. Easily explore the collective animal behaviour.

  • Step Four

    Export

    Download your dataset with the extracted features in .csv format for further analysis.

  • Last Step

    Hypotheses & Knowledge

    Generate new hypotheses and knowledge from different visualizations and extracted features.
